Welcome to LinguaStream

LinguaStream is a real-time translation app that allows seamless communication across language barriers. With our AI-powered translation, you can host or join conversations in multiple languages effortlessly.

How to Use LinguaStream:

  1. Choose Your Role: Select whether you want to join as a Host or a Listener.
  2. For Hosts:
    • Type your message in the text area.
    • Click "Send" to broadcast your message.
    • Your message will be automatically translated for all listeners.
  3. For Listeners:
    • Select your preferred language from the dropdown menu.
    • Wait for incoming messages, which will be translated into your chosen language.
